The Filmmaker

About
Shanmukha

Shanmukha KN is a filmmaker and visual artist based in Bengaluru, India — specialising in cinematography, VFX, direction, and editing. A storyteller by instinct, a technician by training.

His journey began with short films and documentaries as early as 2016 at CREO Valley School of Film and Television — shooting stories that mattered with whatever gear was at hand. From corporate films for Devi Sea Foods and Tarantula, to 3D generalist work, news bulletins, and documentary filmmaking — Shanmukha brings a hands-on, versatile approach to every production.
